🎣 The Fishing Game

Welcome to the peaceful waters! You're a fisher trying to catch as many fish as possible before running out of energy. Cast your line, reel in different types of fish, and manage your stamina wisely. Catching fish restores your energy, so keep fishing to stay alive!
Controls:
🎣 Hold SPACEBAR to charge cast power
🎣 Release to cast your line
🎣 Hold SPACEBAR to reel in, release to let the lure sink
Press SPACE to Start
